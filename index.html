What We’re Building

About

Tshering Capital is a boutique initiative in formation. We develop clear, evidence-based perspectives on corporate combinations, capital formation, and special situations. Our goal is to bring the discipline of institutional finance to founder-led and mid-market contexts—without the noise.

Status: Pre-launch. Exploring conversations with executives, investors, and operators who value deep work, confidentiality, and speed.

How We Work

We anchor around three pillars: rigorous research, clean models, and decision-ready narratives. No buzzwords—just analysis you can act on.

Focus Areas

We are actively researching and selectively engaging across:

  • ► Combinations & carve-outs (buy-side / sell-side analysis)
  • ► Capital formation playbooks (equity/debt alternatives, readiness)
  • ► Recapitalizations & special situations (independent options memos)
  • ► Cross-border considerations (structure, currency, regulatory paths)
  • ► ESG-materiality translation (signal vs. noise in real decisions)

Note: We are not soliciting, offering, or executing securities transactions. All materials are for informational and exploratory purposes.
